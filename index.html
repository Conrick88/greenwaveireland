Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should a heat pump be serviced? +
A heat pump should be serviced annually by a qualified engineer. This includes checking system pressure, flow rates, refrigerant integrity, legionella cycle, weather compensation settings and overall system efficiency. Annual servicing is also a condition of most manufacturer warranties.
What is weather compensation and why does it matter? +
Weather compensation (also called Water Law) automatically adjusts the flow temperature based on outdoor temperature. It's the single most important efficiency setting on a heat pump — without it correctly configured, you can lose up to 30% of your system's seasonal efficiency.
Why is my heat pump not heating my home properly? +
Common causes include incorrect weather compensation settings, low system pressure, air locks, Eco mode restricting output, or a misconfigured heating curve. A GreenWave engineer can typically diagnose and resolve this on a single visit.
Is legionella a risk with a heat pump? +
Yes. If your DHW cylinder is set below 50°C without a weekly sterilisation cycle to 60°C, legionella bacteria can multiply to dangerous levels. GreenWave checks and configures your legionella protection as standard on every service visit. Many Irish installations have this incorrectly set.
Do you service Samsung Joule systems? +
Yes — Samsung EHS Gen6 Split and Joule Zenith systems are our primary speciality. We also service Mitsubishi Ecodan, Daikin Altherma and LG Therma V. Our engineers carry manufacturer service manuals and fault code databases for all major brands.
What areas do you cover? +
We primarily cover Dublin and Fingal, including Swords, Malahide, Balbriggan, Blanchardstown, Santry and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm coverage for your location.
